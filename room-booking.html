Short Stay & Volunteer Experience

Spend meaningful time with our elders! We welcome short-term visitors who want to help, learn, and experience life at IBP Old Age Home. Choose a room type, enjoy our facilities, and participate in activities like yoga, storytelling, and music.

Available Rooms & Stay Options

Single Room

Comfortable room for 1 person. Ideal for short stays.

  • Bed & Linen
  • AC / Fan
  • Attached Bathroom
  • Wi-Fi

Double Room

Spacious room for 2 people. Comfortable for couples or friends.

  • 2 Beds
  • AC / Fan
  • Attached Bathroom
  • Wi-Fi

Dormitory

Shared room with multiple beds. Economical option for short-term visitors.

  • Shared Beds
  • Shared Bathroom
  • Wi-Fi
  • Community Lounge

Volunteer Stay

Participate in activities while staying with our community. Donation-based.

  • Shared Room
  • Meals Provided
  • Activities Participation
  • Wi-Fi

Facilities & Amenities

Comfortable living rooms for all residents and visitors.

Nutritious meals provided for residents and short-term visitors.

Participate in yoga, music, storytelling, and cultural activities.
