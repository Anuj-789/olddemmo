Welcome
Matri-pitri Seva Sadnam

Spend meaningful time with elders, volunteer, or donate to support our home.

Mata Pitarula Seva Sadan in Ibrahimpatnam is a charitable old age home dedicated to caring for elderly people who need support, love, and companionship. Our home provides a safe and peaceful environment where senior citizens can live with dignity, comfort, and happiness.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide shelter, nutritious food, medical support, and emotional care to elderly residents. We believe every elder deserves respect, love, and a family-like environment.

Facilities We Provide

  • Comfortable living rooms for residents
  • Healthy vegetarian meals
  • Regular medical checkups
  • Peaceful garden and recreation area
  • Prayer and cultural activities
  • Support from volunteers and staff

Life at Our Home

Residents participate in daily activities such as yoga, storytelling, music sessions, and cultural celebrations. These activities help keep them active, happy, and socially connected.

Support Our Elders

You can make a difference in the lives of our elders by visiting, volunteering, or donating. Your support helps us provide better care and a happier life for our residents.
